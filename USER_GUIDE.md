# 📖 User Guide: How to use Reporting ERP

Welcome to the Reporting ERP! This guide will help you navigate the system and submit your daily reports efficiently.

## 🏠 Your Dashboard
When you log in, you are greeted by your **Dashboard**. 

- **Calendar View**: Shows your report submission status.
  - 🟢 **Green**: Report submitted for this date.
  - 🔴 **Red**: No report found (Missing entry).
  - 🟡 **Yellow**: Weekly Off (No report required).
  - 🟣 **Purple**: You were on Leave.
- **Top Stats**: Quick glance at total reports submitted and late submissions.

## 📝 Submitting a Daily Report
To submit a report for the day:
1.  Click on **Submit Report** in the navigation menu.
2.  **Date**: Select the date you are reporting for. (By default, it shows today's date).
3.  **Report Type**: 
    - **Regular**: Choose this if you worked.
    - **Leave**: Choose this if you were on leave.
4.  **Shift**: Select your working hours.
5.  **Tasks**: Fill in the numbers for the tasks you completed (e.g., Videos Produced, Articles Written).
6.  **Notes**: Add any additional comments or context for your work.
7.  Click **Submit**.

> [!TIP]
> **Late Submissions**: If you submit a report for a past date, it will be flagged as a "Late Submission". Try to submit your reports daily!

## 📅 Managing Weekly Offs
Your weekly off (e.g., Sunday) is preset by the administrator. On these days, the calendar will show a yellow background. You don't need to submit reports for these days unless you worked extra.

## 🔍 Viewing Past Reports
You can click on any green date in the calendar to see a preview of what you submitted for that day.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting
- **Cannot Log In?**: Contact your team lead or administrator to reset your password.
- **Missing a Field?**: If your team requires a specific field that isn't there, ask the administrator to add a "Dynamic Field" for your team.
